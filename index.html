ABOUT HACKATHON 2023

  A hackathon is an event where programmers, designers, and tech enthusiasts come together to collaborate intensively on software or hardware projects within a limited time frame,
                    usually ranging from 24 to 48 hours.Participants form teams to solve real-world challenges,often proposed by companies,organizations, or governments.These events foster innovation, 
                    creativity,and rapid problem-solving, as teams develop prototypes, apps,or solutions,typically focused on themes like technology,social impact, or industry-specific problems.Hackathons 
                     often culminate in presentations or demos, with prizes awarded to the most impactful and innovative solutions.
